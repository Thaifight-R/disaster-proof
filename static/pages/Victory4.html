A reflective item that keeps a person warm by reducing heat loss that would normally occur due to convection (breeze) and thermal radiation (heat radiating off of a person). The lightweight plastic sheeting can additionally be used to alert others since its metallic surface flashes in the sun. Also referred to as Mylar blanket and thermal blanket.

Distress beacon

A tracking transmitter used to aid in the detection and location of emergency survivors. It interfaces with the international search and rescue (SAR) satellite system offered by Cospas-Sarsat to determine positioning. One typically has a lifetime of 10 years. Also known as an emergency position-indicating radiobeacon station.
